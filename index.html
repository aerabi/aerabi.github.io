About

He is a Senior Backend Engineer at JobRad, a Docker Captain, Snyk Ambassador, and a CNCF community organizer in Freiburg. He founded the Black Forest Docker community and has organized a dozen+ meetups and workshops. His background is in mathematics and logic; he studied Pure Mathematics and researched logic and programming language theory before moving full‑time into software engineering.
